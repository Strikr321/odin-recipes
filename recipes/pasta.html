Pasta

Description

This pasta will be a mixture of grilled chicken, some seasoning, and organic wheat pasta.
This will be a good choice for high protein and low carbs and calories for a diet.

  • Organic Pasta
  • Grilled Chicken Breast
  • Onion Powder
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Garlic Powder
  1. Boil the pasta in a pot of hot water until cooked
  2. Mix your pasta sauce in a separate pot and add the seasonings
  3. Drain the pasta sauce in a separate pot and add the seasonings
  4. Grill your chicken and mix it into the pasta sauce
  5. Serve onto a plate and enjoy!
