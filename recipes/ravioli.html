Ravioli

This ravioli will be a great meal to make if you invite a friend over, have a date, or just want to have some comfort food at home.
It's quite simple and easy to make, and will leave you craving more.

  • Spinach
  • Ravioli
  • Garlic Powder
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  1. First, add the ravioli to a pot of boiling water
  2. Next, add salt to the water with ravioli, and drain it after
  3. Cook spinach in a separate pan and add to the ravioli
  4. Add garlic powder, and any other seasoning you may want
  5. Plate and serve!
